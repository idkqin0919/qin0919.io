Origin

A rejection of the constant stream.

Narrative was born from a simple observation: the digital landscape has become exceedingly noisy. The modern web prioritizes speed and volume over depth and reflection.

We set out to build a platform that strips away the extraneous. No intrusive notifications, no infinite scrolls, no cluttered sidebars. Just the text, elegant typography, and the space necessary to absorb complex ideas.
